About

The Kenya Blockchain and crypto confrence (KBCC) is an established gathering of industry experts, thought leaders, and enthusiasts who are passionate about the transformative potential of blockchain, web3 and cryptocurrencies. KBCC marks a pivotal moment in the intersection of technology and economic developement on the African continent.

As a nexus of ideas, innovatoins, and collaborative efforts, the conference explores the transformative potential of blockchain technology in propelling Africa towards economic properity. The confrence brings together a diverse array of participants to discuss and showcase advancements in blockchain technology, its applications, and its role in shaping Africa's economic landscape.
